Acoustic Guitar Structure

A basic acoustic guitar structure is divided into 11 different parts or elements:
The body, waist, neck, headstock, soundhole, bridge, nut, fretboard which is divided into frets
(in this example, there are 20) and upon which lie the strings, which can be loosened or tightened with tuners.
